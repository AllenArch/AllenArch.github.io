About Allen

I'm a lifelong coder and storyteller who's been immersed in technology since childhood. At 11 years old, I started programming on the TI-99/4A, which led me to assembly language on the Apple II, working with the 6502 and 65816 processors. By 18, just two weeks out of high school, I began my career as a video game programmer, contributing to titles like Rock N' Roll Racing, The Game Genie, and Wing Commander on the SNES...
